Grey Wolf

A pack of wolves can range from 2 to over 30 wolves

  • Scientific Name: Canis Lupus
  • Average Length: Males 5 to 6.5 ft.,Females 4.5 to 6 ft.
  • Average Lifespan: 13 years in the wild.
  • Habitat: Tundra to woodlands, forest, grasslands and deserts.

Wolves are very complex, highly intelligent animals who are caring, playful, and above all devoted to their pack.
